catalog: ar-015

Compilation of sounds from both coasts of the United States and Scotland. It lies somewhere between floating shoegaze, harsh tape excursions, transparent acoustic numbers and glowing zone-out moments... Everyone is painting with a violet brush.

Limited edition of 45 CDs. Risograph covers and inserts printed by Looky Here in Greenfield, MA.
