brandon dunlap - raise your downer gaze


catalog: ar-006

year: 2020

format: cassette


All three tracks were built on improvised loops made using the proccessed piezo pickup of an acoustic bass. I commissioned Sky Semone two states over to record my voice in real time over Zoom and process it through modular synth. I stood in a dead zone so that the signal would be unstable and introduce digital smearing and artifacts.
